National Security Agency (NSA) - The NSA, located in the United States, is responsible for signals intelligence and information assurance. It plays a key role in protecting U.S. government communications and information systems.The NSA is headquartered in Fort Meade, Maryland, United States. NSA
Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) - CISA, part of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, leads the national effort to defend critical infrastructure against cyber threats.CISA's headquarters is located in Arlington, Virginia, United States. CISA
The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) in the United States plays a significant role in cybersecurity, focusing on investigating and combating cybercrimes, protecting critical infrastructure, and collaborating with other agencies and organizations to enhance cybersecurity efforts.The FBI is headquartered in Washington, D.C., United States.
